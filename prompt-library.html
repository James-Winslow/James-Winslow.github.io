Career Development Prompts

CV / Resume Optimizer:
"Here is a resume bullet: [insert bullet]. Rewrite this to be more impactful, using strong action verbs and tailored for a [job title] position. Keep it under 25 words."

Cover Letter Generator:
"Write a brief, 3-paragraph cover letter for a [job title] at [company], based on this resume and job description. Focus on fit, excitement, and brevity."

Email Response Polisher:
"Here’s an email I received: [paste text]. Write a professional response that’s friendly, clear, and shows enthusiasm."

Portfolio & Project Prompts

Idea to Execution Blueprint:
"I have this raw idea: [idea]. Turn it into an execution-ready plan with tools, steps, deadlines, and potential obstacles."

README Writer:
"Here is a summary of my project: [paste summary]. Turn this into a professional GitHub README with sections for Overview, Features, Installation, and Skills Demonstrated."

Break Down Complex Projects:
"Help me break down this complex project into a Gantt chart-style list of tasks, deliverables, and deadlines."

Productivity & Thinking Tools

Workflow Compression:
"Here's my full daily workflow: [insert text]. Compress it into a 2-hour deep work schedule by removing low-value tasks and automating steps."

Pro/Con Decision Assistant:
"You are a decision coach. Help me evaluate this: [decision]. Create a Pro/Con list with short- and long-term implications."

Meeting Summary & Action Extractor:
"Here’s a transcript: [paste]. Summarize the meeting, extract action items, and assign follow-ups as if you were a team lead."

Creative Output Prompts

LinkedIn / Blog Post Generator:
"Turn this idea [insert idea] into a compelling LinkedIn post that mixes storytelling, value, and a touch of personality."

Content Calendar Generator:
"Turn this idea [insert topic] into 30 days of cross-platform content, with hooks, CTAs, visual ideas, and post types."

Presentation Slide Planner:
"I need to present [topic]. Give me a slide-by-slide outline with key points, visual ideas, and story flow."

Research & Learning Prompts

Master a Concept Quickly:
"You are a world-class educator. Teach me [concept] using analogies and simple examples until I deeply understand."

Speed Up Research:
"You are my research assistant. Summarize the top insights, data trends, and sources about [topic]."

Curriculum Designer:
"Outline for me a course entitled 'Saving Money and Investing Wisely' from beginner to expert. Start with the basics of what someone totally new to this topic needs to learn, and walk through the modules and sections before concluding with expert-level financial decision making."

Prompt Engineering Best Practices

  • Use roles: “You are a [type of expert]” to guide tone
  • Add instructions like: “Think step-by-step” or “Don’t stop until the task is complete”
  • Specify format: bullet points, tables, slide outlines
  • Use constraints: word count limits, tone, personas
  • Chain prompts: broad draft → revised output → polished version
